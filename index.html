Meet the Founder

Rise & Heal Collective was founded by a survivor and advocate who understands trauma not only through study, but through lived experience. This movement was born from a journey of standing up for what is right, navigating loss, workplace harm, and choosing healing over silence.

The vision is simple but powerful: no one should feel alone in their pain, and everyone deserves access to compassionate, ethical mental health support.

Our Mission

Rise & Heal Collective exists to raise awareness around mental health and trauma, reduce stigma, and create safe spaces where individuals feel seen, validated, and supported. We bridge survivors with trusted mental health professionals and encourage healing through empathy and community.
