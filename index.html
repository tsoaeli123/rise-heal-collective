Meet Our Founder

A journey from personal experience to compassionate leadership

"Healing begins when we allow ourselves to be seen, heard, and supported."

â€” Founder, Rise & Heal Collective

Rise & Heal Collective was founded by a survivor and advocate who understands trauma not only through academic study, but through lived, personal experience. This movement emerged from a courageous journey of standing up for justice, navigating significant loss, overcoming workplace harm, and ultimately choosing the path of healing over silence.

The vision is profound yet beautifully simple: no individual should ever feel isolated in their pain, and every person deserves compassionate, ethical, and accessible mental health support.

Our Core Mission

We exist to elevate mental health awareness, dismantle stigma, and cultivate safe, nurturing spaces where every individual feels genuinely seen, validated, and supported. We serve as a vital bridge connecting survivors with trusted mental health professionals while fostering healing through authentic empathy and community strength.

What We Stand For

Our core principles guide every interaction and initiative

Empathy First

We practice active listening without judgment, validating every experience with genuine compassion and deep understanding.

Safe Spaces

Emotional safety, confidentiality, and trust form the foundation of every interaction within our community.

Dignified Healing

Every story matters. We honor each person's journey with the utmost respect and preserve dignity throughout.

Professional Excellence

We maintain rigorous standards for ethical referrals, connecting individuals only with qualified, trauma-informed professionals.

Community Strength

Healing thrives in connection. We believe in collective resilience and the power of shared experiences.

Growth & Hope

We champion the human capacity for transformation, celebrating every step toward healing and wholeness.
